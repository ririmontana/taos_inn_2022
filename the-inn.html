The Perfect Stop 

For Your Yellowstone Trip

Built in 2016, Tao's Inn is located in West Yellowstone, the western entrance of the Yellowstone National Park. We have free parking areas for your bikes, cars, or even trailer. WiFi coverage can be found all over the property. AC and streaming TV available in every room.  With walking distance to downtown and other tourist attractions such as Grizzly & Wolf Discovery Center and Yellowstone Aerial Adventures- Zipline Adventure Park. ,Tao's Inn will be your convenient and affordable stay while visiting the Yellowstone National Park!

We are right next to Yellowstone Avenue, get on the road and head east then you can reach the Yellowstone National Park Western Entrance within a minute.

Downtown and many other businesses such as gift stores, restaurants, and grocery store are in the north of our property, head north of Electric street for two blocks and you will arrive the heart of this lovely little town.

We will provide every guest with a map of the town and the national park so you are not likely to get lost here in the wild, just kidding, road system here is straight forward and super easy compared to big cities, feel free to ask us any questions regardless whether you are staying with us or not, always happy to help.
